Best Speech Ever

Blonde Soldier:

Yes, I’ve heard.
Kills men by the hundreds, and if he were here he’d consume the English with fireballs from his eyes and bolts of lightning from his arse.
I am William Wallace, and I see a whole army of my countrymen here in defiance of tyranny.
You have come to fight as free men, and free men you are.
What would you do with that freedom?
Will you fight?
